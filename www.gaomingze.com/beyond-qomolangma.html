   Introduction:
Beyond Qomolangma is a 3D interactive website adapted from the real-life accounts of Chen Min, a national-level Chinese mountaineer. Told from a first-person perspective, it immerses viewers in the peril and grandeur of climbing Mount Everest, blending interactive storytelling and visual experience to reveal the awe-inspiring beauty and resilience behind each step toward the summit.
   Main Part:
The entire website uses the climber's altitude as a clue to showcase the entire climbing process. Each camp's altitude serves as a node, where interactive scenes are set up.
